Mission

This website has been created in order to increase the client of Music Centre. We provided a lot of information so that clients are able to understand and join us.

Success Evaluation

In order to measure the success of the website, we will count the number of visitors and how many people take part in the Music Centre.

Target Audience

The group age aimed by the website is from 6 years old to 70+. The design of the page will make the navigation very easy for younger and older users, so that everyone can use the website without having troubles to find the products. The colors are light in order to attract young audience.

Content

Home Page: this page will provide a menu bar.
Members: this page will tell people many of the privileges they can enjoy when they become members.
Sponsors: this page will let visitors know the sponsors.
Events: this page will show when the activities are hold.
Bulletins: this page will provide a lot of information about the activites.
Contact Us: this page will display the contact details of Music Centre.
Our story: this page will show a brief history by Jean Datnall.
